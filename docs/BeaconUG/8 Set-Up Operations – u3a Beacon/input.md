The settings within these areas establish the working environment of
Beacon for your u3a, and until your site has been set up no other
members should be given access.

The configuration of these sections is crucial to the success of your
u3a’s use of Beacon. If ever you have any doubts or queries please
consult your Beacon Supporter.

Further information can be found in the **Beacon** **Live** **Site**
**‘What** **To** **Do** **Now’** document that was sent to your Site
Administrator when your site was created.

Once set up, most options won’t need to be changed unless any of your
working methods change, although all will need to be confirmed for a new
system installation.

*<u>Note:</u>*

*There* *are* *some* *configuration* *options* *for* *your* *system*
*that* *can* *only* *be* *changed* *by* *the* *Beacon* *Support* *Team.*
*These* *options* *include* *the* *starting* *date* *for* *the*
*Financial* *and* *Membership* *years,* *PayPal* *enablement,* *Custom*
*membership* *fields* *and* *terminology* *aliases* *for* *Groups* *and*
*Group* *Leaders.*

*There* *is* *information* *on* *the* *Site* *Record* *at* *the* *end*
*of* *this* *article* [*Demo* *System* *Getting*
*Started*](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007514178)

*Raise* *a* *ticket* *with* *the* [***<u>Ongoing Help
team</u>***](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007478557-Open-a-Support-Ticket)
*to* *request* *a* *change* *in* *this* *area.*
