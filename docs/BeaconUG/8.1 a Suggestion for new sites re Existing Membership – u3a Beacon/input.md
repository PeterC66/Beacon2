**8.1** **a** **Suggestion** **for** **new** **sites** **re**
**Existing** **Membership**

> <img src="./1iz5axtl.png"
> style="width:0.41667in;height:0.41667in" />John Alexander Follow 9
> months ago · Updated

If you have not entered the members’ payment data into the finance
section of the template, Beacon will not know who has paid until you do
the first membership renewal. However it is useful to have a way in
Beacon to tell who has paid for your current year without having to go
through old records.

The best way to do this is to create a poll called e.g. Paidxxyy where
xxyy is the membership year (e.g. 24-25) or something similar. You then
need to add all the paid members to the poll.

For guidance on how to do this go to the [<u>User Guide</u> <u>4.1 The
Membership
List</u>](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007301057)(4.1.c)
for how to select members from the membership list and then 4.1d for how
to add them to a poll.

Once you have done a full membership renewal you can delete this poll.

N.B. Please note that this POLL will not automatically update.
