**6.1** **Emails**<img src="./mz2jhkeu.png"
style="width:2.05208in;height:0.38542in" />

> Back

Emails may be sent from many places within Beacon, e.g. the Members
List, Group Member lists and the U3A Offices page. All email recipients
are blind copied, so they will not see the other recipients.

*Note:* *the* *email* *operations* *that* *you* *can* *perform* *may*
*be* *different* *to* *those* *described* *below,* *according* *to*
*the* *access* *that* *has* *been* *allocated* *to* *your* *Role* *by*
*your* *U3A.*

Sending Emails

From any list of members, select the names that you wish to send an
email to and proceed as described in
[**<u>section</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007380438)
[**<u>6.1.1</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007380438).

Standard Messages

If you send similar messages regularly they can be saved as standard
message templates and recalled for future use as described in
[**<u>section
6.1.2</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007318777).

Email Delivery

You can check upon the progress of emails sent by clicking **Email**
**delivery** on the Home page as described in
[**<u>section</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007318757)
[**<u>6.1.3</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007318757).

Email Unblocker

The Email Unblocker utility is accessible only to the System
Administrator; [**<u>see
6.1.4</u>**](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007380498).
