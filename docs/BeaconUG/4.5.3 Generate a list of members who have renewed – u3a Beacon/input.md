The **Membership** **Renewals** main menu entry will list those members
who have yet to renew and this can be used, typically, to contact those
members.

Unfortunately there is no one-screen way to generate a list of members
who have renewed - to email them to say thank you for example.

A one-off way round this is to create a **Poll** and populate it with
members who are due to renew. When listing **Members** this poll can be
selected and **Negate** **poll** ticked to show renewed members.

The above video goes through the following steps: -

Log in as Membership Secretary (or admin)

Click on **Poll** and add a poll called "**Not** **renewed**". If it
already exists click on **clear** **all** **assignments**

From the home screen click on **Membership** **renewals** then scroll to
the bottom and click the blue Select and then Select All

*Do* *NOT* click on Do with selected yet - you don't want to renew
everyone. From the drop down select **Add** **to** **poll**. A
**-select** **poll-** dropdown menu now appears where **Not**
**renewed** can be selected. Now click **Do** **with** **selected**

From the main menu select **Members** and filter by **Poll** \> **Not**
**renewed**. Tick **Negate** **poll**

Be aware the poll is not updated automatically so the above will need to
be repeated as members renew or join. Make sure the poll is cleared
first.

This approach can also be used to email members who *have* *renewed* and
copy in other members such as the Membership Secretary or Treasurer etc.
