**4.5.4** **An** **easy** **way** **to** **send** **a** **batch** **of**
**Renewal** **Confirmation** **messages** **to** **Renewed** **Members**

> <img src="./lvdw5wxe.png"
> style="width:0.41667in;height:0.41667in" />Lyn Bossons Follow 6 months
> ago · Updated

**Please** **note** **that** **this** **procedure** **should** **only**
**be** **carried** **out** **by,** **or** **in** **collaboration**
**with** **the** **Membership** **Secretary,** **as** **it** **marks**
**membership** **cards** **as** **having** **been** **printed.**

> 1\. Before renewing a batch of members, select **Membership**
> **cards** on the Home page.
>
> 2\. If there are no members listed, proceed to step 3. If there are
> members listed, select 'do with selected' (all members should have
> already been selected), and when asked to Confirm, click the 'Mark'
> option. You will need to save the PDF created as well if your u3a uses
> membership cards – delete it if you aren’t using it.
>
> 3\. Once you have renewed a batch of members, select **Membership**
> **cards**; this time you will see a list of members who have recently
> renewed.
>
> 4\. Select 'email only' in the selection drop-down menu.
>
> 5\. Where it states 'Download cards', click the dropdown arrow to
> change to 'Send email', then click 'Do with selected'.
>
> 6\. Select the standard message that you have created to confirm
> renewal (or see below for an example). 7. You can either now repeat
> this for those without email and select 'Send letter', or print the
> cards (if
>
> required) for those on email and do the letters later.
>
> 8\. If you are **not** issuing cards, click 'Mark' (as described in
> step 2). If you **are** issuing cards you will need to ensure they are
> downloaded and saved for printing **before** you click 'Mark'.

Example of a standard renewal confirmation email message:

> Dear \#FAM,
>
> Thank you for renewing your \#U3ANAME membership.
>
> <img src="./xrlnwl5o.png"
> style="width:1.125in;height:0.47892in" />Member: \#TITLE \#FORENAME
> \#SURNAME
>
> **Help**
>
> Membership number: \#MEMNO
>
> Membership type: \#MEMCLASS<img src="./wrgy4jqn.png"
> style="width:0.1875in;height:0.18726in" />
>
> Fee Paid: \#FEE (per member)
>
> Valid until: \#RENEW
>
> **\[Please** **note** **that** **the** **token** **names** **need**
> **to** **be** **capitalised** **and** **that** **there** **should**
> **not** **be** **a** **space** **between** **the** **hash** **and**
> **the** **name\]**
>
> **It** **is** **easiest** **to** **click** **on** **the** **Token**
> **as** **it** **is** **then** **inserted** **correctly.**
