Faculties may be used to group together similar Interest Groups.

The list of current faculties is displayed by clicking **Faculties** on
the Home Page.

Press **edit** to change a faculty name and **Delete** to remove the
faculty.

To add a new faculty; enter the faculty name under **Add** **new**
**faculty** and press **Save**.

Faculties can be used to narrow down searches in the **Groups** **List**
page.
