**10.2.2** **Viewing** **your** **Interest** **Groups**

> <img src="./l0t0vrts.png"
> style="width:0.41667in;height:0.41667in" /><img src="./ixzvwhby.png"
> style="width:0.15625in;height:0.15625in" />Graeme Bunting Follow 1
> year ago · Updated

If your u3a has enabled it, you may view information about the Interest
Groups in your u3a by signing in to the **Members** **Portal** as
described in
[10.2](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007368138) and
clicking *(your* *u3a)* **u3a** **groups**

The information displayed in the Members Portal may be more detailed
than that in the 'public' Groups List, depending on the settings used by
your u3a.

> Click on any Group to view information about the Group
>
> <img src="./iwffioo5.png"
> style="width:1.125in;height:0.47892in" />**Help**

Groups that you belong to are indicated
**MEMBER**<img src="./kowiqzbu.png"
style="width:7.02083in;height:3.625in" /><img src="./pz33nif5.png"
style="width:5.20833in;height:2.48958in" /><img src="./qoutxrp2.png" style="width:5.25in;height:2.46875in" />

You can add yourself to a Group by clicking **Join** **group**

You can leave a Group by clicking **Leave** **group**

When joining or leaving a Group you will be prompted to confirm the
operation and the Group Leader will be notified by email.

<img src="./ztqe21rp.png"
style="width:5.86458in;height:2.16667in" /><img src="./gvs01cqg.png"
style="width:0.1875in;height:0.18725in" />

> *Note:* *You* *may* *need* *to* *scroll* *downwards* *to* *see* *the*
> ***Join*** *&* ***Leave*** *prompts.*
