**9.5.1** **What** **to** **do** **with** **Beacon** **records**
**when** **leaving** **Beacon**

> <img src="./ndqzmx3v.png"
> style="width:0.41667in;height:0.41667in" />Lyn Bossons Follow 10
> months ago · Updated

Should your u3a have decided to leave Beacon you are strongly advised
to:

> 1\. Refer to article [<u>4.2.2 Deleting members including GDPR
> compliance</u>](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360019707938-4-2-2-Deleting-members-including-GDPR-compliance)
>
> 2\. Backup all data to an Excel file: please refer to article [<u>9.5
> Data Export and
> Backup</u>](https://u3abeacon.zendesk.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360007304557-9-5-Data-Export-and-Backup)
>
> 3\. Inform the Beacon Team by emailing info@beacon.u3a.org.uk. A
> member of the team will contact you and explain the procedure for
> leaving Beacon.
>
> 4\. You should also view the Beacon Terms and Conditions.
