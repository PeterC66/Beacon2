# 51. Finance Accounts

Finance Accounts is where you set up and manage the accounts that Beacon2 uses
to track your u3a's money. Most u3as will have at least a current account and
possibly a savings account or petty cash account.

To open this page, click **Finance accounts** in the **Set Up** section of the
Home page.

![The Finance Accounts page](images/51-finance-accounts.png)

---

## The account list [A]

The main table shows all your finance accounts with:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Account name** | The name of the account |
| **Active** | Whether the account is currently in use |
| **Balance B/F** | The balance brought forward from before Beacon2 |
| **Configure** | A link to open the account's detailed settings |

---

## Balance brought forward [B]

When you first set up Beacon2, you may need to enter an opening balance for each
account. Click the **Balance B/F** field to edit it inline — type the amount and
press **Enter** or click away to save.

---

## Adding a new account [C]

Use the **Add new account** form at the bottom of the list. Enter the account
name and click **Add**. The new account appears in the list ready for
configuration.

---

## Toggling active / inactive [D]

Click the **Active** toggle to switch an account between active and inactive.
Inactive accounts do not appear in dropdowns when adding transactions, but their
history is preserved and you can still view past transactions.

---

## Configuring an account [E]

Click the **Configure** link next to an account to open the **Configure Account**
page. Here you can adjust:

| Setting | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Account name** | Shown at the top. If the account is locked (because it has transactions), the name is read-only. |
| **Pending transactions mode** | Choose **Disable** (no pending transactions), **Optional** (transactions can be marked as pending), or **By type** (certain payment types are automatically pending). If you choose By type, a list of payment type checkboxes appears so you can select which ones. |
| **Enable Refunds** | Tick this to allow refund transactions to be created against this account. |
| **Group B/F** | Tick this if you want to enter brought-forward balances at the group level for this account. |

At the bottom of the Configure Account page there is a link to
**Payment Method Defaults** — see
[Section 59: Payment Method Defaults](59-payment-method-defaults.md) for details.
