# 24. Open Meetings

Open Meetings are events that are not tied to any specific interest group.
They cover u3a-wide activities such as AGMs, social events, guest lectures
and other gatherings that any member can attend.

## Where to manage Open Meetings

Open Meetings are now managed directly on the
[Events page](23-calendar.md) (route `/calendar`). Select the
**Open Meetings and Other** filter and pick an event type — an event-type
dropdown appears, along with an **Add Events** form (supporting recurrence)
and bulk-delete controls. Editing an individual open meeting is done on
that event's **Event Record** page, reached by clicking the date in its row.

The standalone `/calendar/open-meetings` page has been retired; any old
bookmark redirects to the Events page with the right filter preselected.

## How Open Meetings appear in the Calendar

Open meeting events are shown alongside group events on the Events page.
They appear with the event-type name in place of a group name, making them
easy to identify. Events marked as **Private** (Exclude from public
calendar) are excluded from any public-facing calendar (such as the Members
Portal or website) but still appear in the administrative Events list.
