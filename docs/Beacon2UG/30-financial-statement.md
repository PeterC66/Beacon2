# 30. Financial Statement

The Financial Statement gives you a formal summary of your u3a's income and
expenditure for a financial year — the kind of report you need for your AGM,
your trustees, or your independent examiner.

To open it, click **Financial statement** on the **Home** page.

![Financial Statement screen](images/30-financial-statement.png)

---

## Selecting accounts and year

### Account selection [A]

Tick the checkboxes next to the accounts you want to include in the report.
Use the **All accounts** checkbox to select every account at once — this gives
you a consolidated view across all your u3a's finances.

### Financial year [B]

Choose the **Financial year** from the dropdown. The statement covers the full
year from your u3a's financial year start date to its end date.

---

## Pending transactions warning [C]

If there are any **pending transactions** in the selected accounts for the
chosen year, Beacon2 displays a warning banner. Pending transactions are
**excluded** from the statement figures.

This is by design — pending transactions have not yet been confirmed, so they
should not appear in formal financial reports. If you want them included, go
to the ledger and confirm them first (see [Section 34](34-pending-transactions.md)).

---

## Receipts & Payments [D]

The main body of the statement is the **Receipts & Payments** table, split into
two halves:

### Income categories

All income categories are listed with their totals for the year. This shows you
where your money came from — subscriptions, group fees, donations, and so on.
A **total income** row sums everything up.

### Expenditure categories

All expenditure categories are listed with their totals. This shows you where
your money went — room hire, printing, postage, etc. A **total expenditure**
row sums everything up.

### Net Surplus / Deficit

At the bottom of the Receipts & Payments section, Beacon2 calculates the **Net
Surplus** (if income exceeded expenditure) or **Net Deficit** (if expenditure
exceeded income) for the year.

---

## Balance Sheet [E]

Below the Receipts & Payments, the **Balance Sheet** section shows:

| Row | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Opening balance** | The balance at the start of the financial year |
| **Total receipts** | All income during the year |
| **Total payments** | All expenditure during the year |
| **Closing balance** | The balance at the end of the financial year |

When you have selected **All accounts**, the Balance Sheet gives you the overall
financial position of your u3a. When individual accounts are selected, it shows
the position for those accounts only.

---

## Downloading the statement [F]

Click **Download as Excel** to export the statement as a spreadsheet file. This
is useful for:

- Sending to your independent examiner or accountant.
- Presenting at your AGM.
- Keeping an offline copy for your records.
