# 18. Group Record -- Members

The **Members** tab of the Group Record shows everyone who belongs to the
group -- both full members and those on the waiting list. From here you can
add and remove members, mark group leaders, and perform bulk actions such as
sending emails or downloading a list.

![The Group Record -- Members tab](images/18-group-record-members.png)

---

## The members table

The table lists every person associated with the group. Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Select** | A checkbox for selecting members for bulk actions |
| **No.** [A] | The member's membership number |
| **Name** [B] | The member's full name (click to open their Member Record) |
| **Town** [C] | The member's town |
| **Email** [D] | The member's email address |
| **Telephone** [E] | The member's telephone number |
| **Status** [F] | Whether the person is a joined member or on the waiting list |
| **Leader** [G] | A flag indicating whether this person is a group leader |
| **Waiting date** [H] | The date the person was added to the waiting list (blank for joined members) |

You can sort the table by clicking any column header. Click again to reverse
the sort order.

---

## Filtering the members list

Above the table you will find two filter checkboxes:

- **Show joined members** [I] -- when ticked, members who have fully joined
  the group are displayed. This is ticked by default.
- **Show waiting members** [J] -- when ticked, members on the waiting list
  are also displayed. Tick both checkboxes to see everyone.

---

## Adding a member to the group

There are two ways to add someone:

### By name

Start typing a member's name into the **Add member** search box [K]. A
dropdown of matching names appears -- click the person you want to add.
They are immediately added to the group.

### By membership number

Type a membership number directly into the **Membership number** field [L]
and press **Add**. This is useful if you have a membership number to hand
and want to avoid searching by name.

> **Tip:** If the group has a maximum membership set and the waiting list is
> enabled, new members added when the group is full will be placed on the
> waiting list automatically.

---

## Marking members as leaders

To make someone a group leader, click the **Leader** toggle [G] in their
row. Group leaders are shown in the Groups List and receive notifications
(if the "Notify leader" option is enabled on the Details tab).

Click the toggle again to remove the leader flag.

---

## Removing a member

To remove a member from the group, click the **Remove** button at the
right-hand end of their row. You will be asked to confirm before the member
is removed.

Removing a member from a group does not delete their membership record -- it
simply removes their association with this group.

---

## Selecting members

Use the checkboxes in the left-hand column to select individual members, or
use the shortcut buttons above the table:

| Button | What it selects |
|--------|-----------------|
| **All** [M] | Every member currently displayed |
| **Clear** [N] | Deselects all previously ticked members |
| **Email only** [O] | Only members who have an email address on file |
| **Without email** [P] | Only members who do *not* have an email address |

---

## Bulk actions

Once you have selected one or more members, use the action buttons below the
table:

### Send email

Click **Send email** [Q] to open the email composer addressed to the
selected members (see [Section 35: Sending Emails](35-sending-emails.md)).

### Download

Click **Download** [R] to generate a file of the selected members. You can
choose between:

- **Excel** -- a spreadsheet you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or
  similar.
- **PDF** -- a formatted document suitable for printing.

Before the download is created, you can choose which fields to include --
tick or untick columns to tailor the output to your needs.
