# 56. Custom Fields

Custom Fields let you add up to four optional free-text fields to every member
record. These are handy for capturing information that is specific to your u3a
and does not fit into the standard member fields — for example, "Emergency
Contact", "Dietary Requirements", or "How did you hear about us?".

To open this page, click **Custom fields** in the **Set Up** section of the
Home page.

![The Custom Fields page](images/56-custom-fields.png)

---

## How it works [A]

The page shows four label fields, one for each custom field. To enable a
custom field, simply type a label into the box. To disable a field, clear
its label so the box is empty.

| Field | What to do |
|-------|------------|
| **Custom field 1 label** | Enter a label (up to 60 characters) to enable this field, or leave blank to hide it |
| **Custom field 2 label** | Enter a label (up to 60 characters) to enable this field, or leave blank to hide it |
| **Custom field 3 label** | Enter a label (up to 60 characters) to enable this field, or leave blank to hide it |
| **Custom field 4 label** | Enter a label (up to 60 characters) to enable this field, or leave blank to hide it |

Click **Save** when you are done.

---

## Where custom fields appear [B]

Once a custom field has a label, it will appear in two places:

- **Member Record** — the field is shown on the member's details page, where
  you can enter and edit the value
- **Members List** — you can search and filter by custom field values

If you clear a label later, the field disappears from the member record and
the Members List, but any data already entered is preserved in case you
re-enable the field.

> **Tip:** Keep your labels short and clear. Other users need to understand at
> a glance what information should go in each field.
