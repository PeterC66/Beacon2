# 55. Polls

Polls are simple flags that you can assign to members for filtering and bulk
actions. They are useful for tracking things like "Interested in Day Trips",
"Volunteers for Open Day", or any other yes/no attribute that does not fit
neatly into the standard member fields.

To open this page, click **Poll** in the **Set Up** section of the Home page.

![The Polls page](images/55-polls.png)

---

## The poll list [A]

The main table shows all polls with:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Name** | The name of the poll |
| **Description** | A short explanation of what the poll is for |
| **Member can set** | Whether members can add or remove themselves from this poll via the Members Portal |
| **Members** | How many members are currently assigned to this poll |
| **Edit** | Click to edit the poll's details |
| **Delete** | Click to delete the poll |

---

## Creating a poll [B]

1. Click **Create new poll**.
2. Enter a **Name** (required) — keep it short and descriptive.
3. Enter a **Description** — this is required if the **Member can set** option
   is ticked, because members will see this text on the portal and need to
   understand what they are opting into.
4. Tick **Member can set** if you want members to be able to assign or remove
   themselves from this poll through the Members Portal.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Editing a poll [C]

Click **Edit** next to a poll to change its name, description, or the
**Member can set** setting. Changes take effect immediately after saving.

---

## Deleting a poll [D]

Click **Delete** next to a poll. You will be asked to confirm because deleting
a poll **removes all member assignments** — every member who was assigned to
that poll will be unassigned. This cannot be undone.

---

## Clearing all assignments [E]

If you want to keep the poll but remove everyone from it, click
**Clear all assignments**. This unassigns all members from the poll without
deleting the poll itself. You will be asked to confirm before proceeding.

---

## Assigning members to polls

Members are not assigned to polls from this page. Instead, you assign members
from the **Members List** using bulk actions:

1. Go to the Members List.
2. Select one or more members using the checkboxes.
3. Use the bulk action to assign or remove them from a poll.

You can also filter the Members List by poll to see which members are currently
assigned.

> **Tip:** Polls are a lightweight way to segment your membership. If you find
> yourself needing more complex groupings, consider whether a membership class
> or group would be more appropriate.
