# 52. Finance Categories

Finance Categories let you classify your u3a's income and expenditure so you can
see where money is coming from and where it is going. Common categories include
Subscriptions, Room Hire, Speaker Fees, and Refreshments.

To open this page, click **Finance categories** in the **Set Up** section of the
Home page.

![The Finance Categories page](images/52-finance-categories.png)

---

## The category list [A]

The table shows each category with its **name** and whether it is **active**.

---

## Renaming a category [B]

1. Click the **Rename** button next to the category you want to change.
2. The name becomes an editable text field.
3. Type the new name.
4. Click **Save** to confirm, or **Cancel** to discard your change.

---

## Toggling active / inactive [C]

Click the **Active** toggle to switch a category between active and inactive.
Inactive categories no longer appear in dropdowns when creating transactions,
but any existing transactions tagged with that category are unaffected.

---

## Adding a new category [D]

Type a name in the **Add new category** field at the bottom of the list and
click **Add**. The new category appears in the table and is active by default.

---

## Deleting a category [E]

Click **Delete** next to a category to remove it. You can only delete a category
that is not **locked** — a category becomes locked once transactions have been
recorded against it, because deleting it would leave those transactions without
a category.

> **Tip:** If you no longer need a category but it is locked, set it to
> **inactive** instead. It will stop appearing in dropdowns but the historical
> data is preserved.
