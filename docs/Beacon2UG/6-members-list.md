# 6. The Members List

The Members List is your main membership directory -- the place you will visit most
often in Beacon2. It shows every member in a sortable, filterable table, and gives
you tools to search, select, and take action on groups of members at once.

![The Members List page](images/members-list.png)

---

## Opening the Members List

From the **Home** page, click **Members**. The Members List loads with your default
filters applied (usually showing **Current** members).

---

## Filtering the list

The filter controls at the top of the page let you narrow down which members are
displayed.

### Status checkboxes [A]

Tick one or more **Status** boxes to choose which members appear -- for example
**Current**, **Lapsed**, **Resigned**, or **Deceased**. You can tick several at
once to see members across multiple statuses.

### Class dropdown [B]

Select a **Class** from the dropdown to show only members belonging to that
membership class (e.g. Individual, Joint, Associate).

### Poll selector [C]

Choose a **Poll** from the dropdown to display members who belong to that poll.
Tick the **Negate** checkbox next to it to flip the filter -- this shows members
who are *not* in the selected poll, which is handy for finding people you still
need to contact.

### Payment method filter [D]

Choose a **Payment method** from the dropdown to display members whose most recent
membership fee was paid using that method (e.g. BACS, cheque, cash).

### Quick Find search [E]

Type into the **Quick Find** box and press **Search** to find members by name,
"known as" name, street, town, postcode, telephone, mobile, or membership number.
Partial matches work -- for example, typing "Smith" will find Smithson as well as
Smith.

> **Tip:** If the **include criteria below** checkbox is ticked, Quick Find only
> searches within the members already shown by your other filters. Leave it
> unticked to search across all members regardless of the current filters.

When you have finished searching, click **Cancel Search** to return to the normal
list view.

### Custom field search [F]

If your u3a has set up custom fields (see [Section 56](56-custom-fields.md)),
you can search by those values here.

### Alphabet navigation [G]

Click any letter **A -- Z** in the alphabet bar above the table to jump straight
to members whose surname starts with that letter. This is especially useful in
large membership lists.

---

## Sorting the table

Click any **column header** to sort the table by that column. Click the same
header again to reverse the sort order. For example, clicking **Name** sorts
alphabetically by surname; clicking **Address** sorts by house number and street.

---

## Selecting members

Before you can perform a bulk action, you need to select one or more members.

- **Individual selection** -- tick the checkbox in the left-hand column next to
  each member's name.
- **Bulk selection** -- click **Select** at the top of the checkbox column, then
  choose one of the following:

| Button | What it selects |
|--------|-----------------|
| **All** | Every member currently displayed |
| **Clear All** | Deselects all previously ticked members |
| **Email only** | Only members who have an email address on file |
| **Without email** | Only members who do *not* have an email address |

---

## Bulk actions ("Do with selected")

Once you have selected one or more members, choose an action from the **Do with
selected** dropdown below the table, then click the **Do with selected** button.

| Action | What it does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Send email** | Opens the email composer addressed to the selected members (see [Section 35](35-sending-emails.md)) |
| **Send letter** | Opens the letter composer for the selected members (see [Section 39](39-composing-letters.md)) |
| **Add to poll** | Lets you pick a poll and adds the selected members to it |
| **Download Excel** | Generates an Excel spreadsheet of the selected members |
| **Download PDF** | Generates a PDF document of the selected members |
| **Download email addresses** | Generates a CSV file of email addresses |

### Customising download columns

When you choose one of the download options, Beacon2 lets you select which columns
to include in the output. Tick or untick columns to tailor the download to your
needs -- for example, you might only need names and postcodes for a mailing list.
