# 29. Reconcile Account

Reconciliation is the process of matching your Beacon2 ledger against your bank
statement to make sure everything agrees. This is an important routine task for
any u3a treasurer.

To open this screen, click **Reconcile account** on the **Home** page.

![Reconcile Account screen](images/29-reconcile-account.png)

---

## Getting started

1. **Select an account** [A] — choose the finance account you want to reconcile
   from the dropdown.
2. **Enter the statement end date** [B] — type the closing date shown on your
   bank statement.
3. **Enter the statement balance** [C] — type the closing balance shown on your
   bank statement.

Beacon2 will display a summary and a list of uncleared transactions.

---

## Balance summary [D]

The balance summary panel shows three key figures:

| Figure | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Cleared balance** | The total of all transactions already marked as cleared in Beacon2 |
| **Statement balance** | The closing balance you entered from your bank statement |
| **Difference** | The gap between the two — your goal is to get this to **zero** |

As you tick transactions in the list below, the **Cleared balance** updates
and the **Difference** recalculates in real time.

---

## Uncleared transactions table [E]

This table shows every transaction in the selected account that has **not yet
been cleared**. Each row has:

- A **checkbox** — tick it to mark the transaction as cleared.
- The transaction **date**, **detail**, **amount**, and other identifying
  information.

Work through your bank statement line by line. For each item on the statement,
find the matching transaction in Beacon2 and tick its checkbox.

---

## Clearing batches [F]

If you have grouped transactions into credit batches (see
[Section 28](28-credit-batches.md)), you can **clear a whole batch with a
single tick**. This is much faster than ticking individual transactions when
your bank statement shows a single lump-sum deposit.

---

## Select all / Clear all [G]

Use the quick-selection controls to:

- **Select all** — tick every uncleared transaction at once. Useful when your
  statement covers everything and you just need to untick a few items.
- **Clear all** — untick every transaction so you can start again.

---

## Finishing the reconciliation

Once the **Difference** is **zero**, click the **Reconcile Account** button [H]
to confirm. Beacon2 will mark all ticked transactions as cleared.

> **Tip:** If the difference is not zero, double-check your statement balance
> and review the uncleared list carefully. Common causes include:
> - A transaction entered in Beacon2 that has not yet appeared on the bank
>   statement (e.g. an uncleared cheque).
> - A bank charge or interest payment that has not been entered in Beacon2.
> - A transposed digit in the statement balance.
