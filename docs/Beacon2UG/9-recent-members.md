# 9. Recent Members

The **Recent Members** screen gives you a quick view of members who have joined
your u3a within the last 30 days. It is especially useful at the start of a new
membership year or after a recruitment drive, when you want to welcome newcomers,
add them to interest groups, or simply keep an eye on new sign-ups.

![The Recent Members page](images/recent-members.png)

---

## Opening the Recent Members screen

From the **Home** page, click **Recent members**. The screen loads showing all
members whose join date falls within the default date range (the last 30 days).

---

## Changing the date range [A]

At the top of the screen you can adjust the **From** and **To** dates to widen
or narrow the window. For example, if you want to see everyone who joined in the
last three months, change the **From** date accordingly and click **Search**.

---

## The recent members table [B]

The table displays one row per recently-joined member with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Select** | Checkbox for selecting members for bulk actions |
| **Membership No** | The member's membership number (click to open their record) |
| **Name** | Full name of the member |
| **Class** | Their membership class (e.g. Individual, Joint) |
| **Status** | Membership status (usually Current for recent joiners) |
| **Joined** | The date the member joined |
| **Address** | The member's address |
| **Telephone** | Contact phone number |

You can sort the table by clicking any **column header**. Click the same header
again to reverse the sort order.

---

## Selecting members [C]

Tick the checkbox next to one or more members to select them for a bulk action.
Use the **Select all** checkbox at the top of the selection column to select
every member in the list.

---

## Bulk actions

Once you have selected one or more members, you can perform the following actions:

### Download names [D]

Click **Download names** to generate a simple text file listing the selected
members' names. This is handy when you need a quick list for a meeting agenda,
a welcome letter, or a committee report.

### Add to group [E]

Click **Add to group** to add the selected members to an interest group. A
**group picker** dialog appears where you can search for and select the group
you want. This saves you having to open each member record individually to add
them to a group.

> **Tip:** Use this feature to add all new members to a "New Members" or
> "Welcome" group so they automatically receive your introductory emails or
> invitations to taster sessions.

---

## Typical workflow

1. After your monthly committee meeting, open **Recent Members** to see who has
   joined since the last meeting.
2. Select all the new members and click **Download names** to produce a list for
   the committee.
3. Select the new members again and click **Add to group** to add them to your
   "New Members Welcome" group.
4. Click on individual member names to check their records are complete --
   especially email addresses and postcodes.
