# 12. Membership Cards

The **Membership cards** screen lets you produce membership cards for your members —
either as downloadable PDFs for printing, or sent directly by email. You can also
export the card data to Excel for use with third-party card printers.

To open it, click **Membership cards** on the Home page.

![Membership cards screen](images/12-membership-cards.png)

---

## Filter modes

Use the filter dropdown [A] at the top of the screen to choose which members to show:

| Mode | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| **Outstanding only** | Members who have not yet had a card printed or emailed for the current period |
| **Poll** | Members who belong to a specific poll (select the poll from the poll dropdown [B]) |
| **Outstanding + Poll** | Combines both — outstanding members within a chosen poll |
| **All current** | Every member with a current (active) status |

### Poll selector

When you choose a poll-based mode (**Poll** or **Outstanding + Poll**), a poll
dropdown [B] appears. Select the poll you want to filter by. This is useful if you
print cards in batches — for example, one batch per renewal month.

### Advance year checkbox

Tick the **Advance year** checkbox [C] to generate cards showing next year's expiry
date instead of the current year. This is handy when you are printing cards towards
the end of the membership year for members who have already renewed for next year.

---

## The member table

The table lists the members matching your chosen filter, with columns for membership
number, name, class, and status. You can:

- **Sort** the table by clicking any column header [D].
- **Select individual members** using the checkboxes on the left [E].
- **Select all** using the checkbox in the header row.

---

## Actions

### Download cards (PDF)

Click **Download cards** [F] to generate a PDF of membership cards for the selected
members.

- Cards are sized **85 × 54 mm** (standard credit-card size).
- **10 cards per A4 page**, arranged in two columns of five.
- Each card includes the member's name, membership number, u3a name, and expiry date.
- A **Code 128 barcode** of the membership number is printed on each card for
  scanning at events or meetings.

> **Tip:** Use Avery-compatible pre-cut card sheets for best results, or print on
> plain card and cut to size.

### Print blank cards

Click **Print blank cards** [G] to generate a PDF of blank card templates — useful
if you need spare cards for handwriting details at a meeting or event.

### Send card by email

Select one or more members and click **Send card by email** [H] to email each
selected member their membership card as a PDF attachment. The member must have a
valid email address on file.

### Download Excel card data

Click **Download Excel** [I] to export the card data (membership number, name,
expiry date, barcode value, etc.) as an Excel spreadsheet. This is useful if you
use an external printing service or want to mail-merge cards in your own template.

---

## Marking cards as printed

After downloading a PDF of cards, a confirmation dialog asks whether you would like
to **Mark as printed** [J].

- If you click **Yes**, the selected members are recorded as having had their card
  printed. They will no longer appear in the **Outstanding only** filter.
- If you click **No**, the members remain marked as outstanding so you can reprint
  them later.

> **Tip:** If you are doing a test print, click **No** so the members stay in the
> outstanding list until you are happy with the final print.

---

## Typical workflow

1. Select **Outstanding only** to see members who need cards.
2. Select all (or pick specific members).
3. Click **Download cards** to get the PDF.
4. Print the cards on card stock.
5. When prompted, click **Mark as printed** to clear them from the outstanding list.
6. For members with email addresses, you can use **Send card by email** instead of
   (or as well as) printing.
