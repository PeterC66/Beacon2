# 7. The Member Record

The Member Record holds everything Beacon2 knows about an individual member --
personal details, contact information, address, membership status, notes, and more.
You can also see which groups the member belongs to, view their financial
transactions, and manage their poll memberships.

![The Member Record page](images/member-record.png)

---

## Opening a Member Record

Click on a member's **name** (shown as a blue link) anywhere in Beacon2 -- most
commonly from the [Members List](6-members-list.md). The full Member Record opens
for viewing and editing.

---

## Personal details [A]

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Title** | Mr, Mrs, Ms, Dr, etc. Required if the member is registered for Gift Aid. |
| **Forenames** | The member's first name(s). |
| **Surname** | Family name. |
| **Known as** | An informal name the member prefers, e.g. "Bill" instead of "William". Used in email merge fields. |
| **Initials** | Auto-generated from forenames; you can edit them manually. |
| **Suffix** | Honours or qualifications, e.g. MBE, PhD. |

---

## Contact details [B]

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Email** | The member's email address. A **Send** button next to it lets you send a quick email directly. |
| **Mobile** | Personal to this member (not shared with a partner). |
| **Telephone** | Home/landline number -- shared with a partner if addresses are linked. |
| **Emergency contact** | Name and number of an emergency contact person. |

---

## Address [C]

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **House no / name** | House number or name. |
| **Street** | Street name. |
| **Town** | Town or city. |
| **County** | County (optional but recommended). |
| **Postcode** | **Required.** Validated on save; stored in uppercase. |

If the member shares an address with a partner, changes to the address fields
update both records automatically.

---

## Membership details [D]

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Status** | Current, Lapsed, Resigned, Deceased, etc. Changing a System User's status to Deceased or Resigned automatically removes their system access. |
| **Class** | The membership class (e.g. Individual, Joint, Associate). |
| **Joined** | Date the member first joined. Set automatically but can be overridden. |
| **Next renewal** | When the member's subscription is next due. Set automatically by the renewal process. |
| **Gift Aid from** | The date from which the member consented to Gift Aid claims. Leave blank if not applicable. Cannot be set to a future date. Press **Cancel** to remove consent. |

---

## Notes and custom fields [E]

A free-text **Notes** area lets you record anything relevant about the member.

If your u3a has defined custom fields (see [Section 56](56-custom-fields.md)),
they appear here as additional fields you can fill in.

---

## Polls [F]

Any polls your u3a has created are shown as checkboxes. Tick or untick a poll to
add or remove the member. See [Section 55: Polls](55-polls.md) for more about
setting up polls.

---

## Partner linking [G]

If two members share the same address, they can be linked as **partners**. This
is a bidirectional link -- updating the address on one record updates the other
automatically.

You can link a partner when adding a new member (see [Section 8](8-add-new-member.md))
or from the Member Record itself.

> **Note:** "Partner" in Beacon2 simply means two members who share an address.
> It does not imply any particular membership class -- Individual members can be
> partners just as Joint members can.

---

## Photo upload [H]

Click the **Choose File** button to upload a photo of the member.

- Accepted formats: **jpg**, **png**, or **gif**
- Maximum file size: **2 MB**
- A square photo (1:1 aspect ratio) works best, especially if you use membership
  cards

---

## Groups tab [I]

For existing members, the **Groups** tab at the bottom of the record shows every
group the member belongs to (or is on the waiting list for). Click a group name
to go directly to that group's record.

Groups that are not currently active are shown in red with a status of
**Inactive**.

---

## Ledger tab [J]

The **Ledger** tab shows the member's financial transaction history -- membership
payments, group fees, refunds, and so on. Click a transaction to open the full
transaction record.

---

## Compact View

Click **Compact View** for a read-only summary of the member's key information
on a single screen. This is useful when you need a quick reference during a phone
call or meeting without the full edit form.

---

## Saving and deleting

- Click **Save** to commit any changes you have made to the record.
- Click **Delete** to permanently remove the member record (if your role permits
  it). Before deleting, the member must first be removed from all groups. In most
  cases it is better to change the member's status to **Lapsed**, **Resigned**, or
  **Deceased** rather than deleting the record outright.
