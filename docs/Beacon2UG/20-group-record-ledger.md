# 20. Group Record -- Ledger

The **Ledger** tab of the Group Record provides a simple way to track income
and expenditure for the group. This is an independent, group-level ledger --
it is separate from the main Finance module and does not affect your u3a's
central financial accounts.

Many groups collect small amounts of money for room hire, refreshments, or
materials. The group ledger gives leaders and administrators a clear record
of where that money has gone.

![The Group Record -- Ledger tab](images/20-group-record-ledger.png)

---

## The ledger table

The table shows all entries for the group, with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** [A] | The date of the transaction |
| **Description** [B] | A short note explaining what the entry is for (e.g. "Room hire -- March", "Subs collected") |
| **In** [C] | The amount received (income) |
| **Out** [D] | The amount spent (expenditure) |
| **Balance** [E] | The running balance after this entry |

Entries are shown in date order, with the most recent at the bottom. The
running balance updates automatically as entries are added.

---

## Adding a ledger entry

Click the **Add Entry** button [F] to create a new row. Fill in:

- **Date** -- the date of the transaction.
- **Description** -- a short explanation of the income or expenditure.
- **In** or **Out** -- enter the amount in the appropriate column. You only
  need to fill in one of these -- leave the other blank.

Click **Save** to add the entry to the ledger.

---

## Editing or deleting entries

Click on an existing entry to edit its details. You can change the date,
description, or amounts. Click **Save** to apply your changes, or
**Cancel** to discard them.

To delete an entry, open it for editing and click **Delete**.

---

## Downloading the ledger

Click **Download Excel** [G] to export the ledger as a spreadsheet. This is
useful for sharing with group members, attaching to reports, or keeping an
offline copy.

---

## How the group ledger relates to Finance

The group ledger is completely independent of the main financial accounts
(see [Section 25: The Financial Ledger](25-financial-ledger.md)). Entries
here do **not** create transactions in your u3a's central books.

If your u3a treasurer needs to record a payment to or from a group in the
main accounts, they would do that separately through the Finance module. The
group ledger is simply a convenient notebook for the group itself.
