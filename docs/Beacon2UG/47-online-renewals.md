# 47. Online Renewals

> **This feature is not yet implemented.**

Online Renewals will allow existing members to renew their membership and pay
online through the Members Portal, in a similar way to how
[Online Joining](41-online-joining.md) works for new members.

When this feature is built, members will be able to:

- Log in to the [Members Portal](42-members-portal.md).
- See that their renewal is due.
- Pay their renewal fee online.
- Have their membership automatically updated to reflect the new renewal period.

This section of the User Guide will be updated with full details — including
step-by-step instructions and screenshots — when the feature becomes available.
