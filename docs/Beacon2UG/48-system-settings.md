# 48. System Settings

System Settings is where you configure the organisation-wide options that affect
how Beacon2 behaves for your u3a. From here you can set your membership year
dates, fees behaviour, contact details, and more.

To open this page, click **System settings** in the **Set Up** section of the
Home page.

![The System Settings page](images/48-system-settings.png)

---

## Membership Cards [A]

Use the **Card colour** picker to choose the background colour for your u3a's
membership cards. You can also control whether members receive their cards by
email along with a confirmation message.

---

## Contact Details [B]

Enter your u3a's public contact information:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Phone** | The main phone number shown on public pages and correspondence |
| **Email** | Your u3a's general email address |
| **Home page** | The URL of your u3a's website |
| **Enquiries email** | Where prospective-member enquiries should be sent |

---

## Membership Year & Fees [C]

This is one of the most important sections — it controls how Beacon2 calculates
renewals, lapses, and fees.

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Year start month / day** | The date your membership year begins (e.g. 1 September) |
| **Fee variation** | Choose **Same** if the fee is the same all year, or **Varies by month** if mid-year joiners pay a reduced rate |
| **Extended membership month** | The month from which new joiners are given membership extending into the following year |
| **Advance renewals (weeks)** | How many weeks before renewal date members are invited to renew |
| **Grace lapse (weeks)** | How many weeks after the renewal date a member is allowed before being lapsed |
| **Non-renewal deletion (years)** | How many years after lapsing before non-renewed members are automatically deleted |
| **Default payment method** | The payment method pre-selected when adding new transactions |

---

## Gift Aid [D]

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Enable Gift Aid** | Turns on Gift Aid tracking across Beacon2 |
| **Show for online renewals** | Whether members see the Gift Aid option when renewing online |

---

## Defaults for New Members [E]

These values are pre-filled when you add a new member, saving time if most of
your members are in the same area:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Default town** | Pre-filled town for new member addresses |
| **Default county** | Pre-filled county for new member addresses |
| **Default STD code** | Pre-filled telephone area code for new members |

---

## Online Payments — PayPal [F]

If your u3a uses PayPal to accept online joining or renewal payments, enter:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **PayPal account email** | The email address linked to your PayPal account |
| **Cancel return URL** | The web page a member is sent to if they cancel the PayPal payment |

---

## Member Record [G]

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Warn on shared address status differences** | Show a warning if two members at the same address have different statuses |
| **Warn on shared address class differences** | Show a warning if two members at the same address have different membership classes |

These warnings help you spot inconsistencies — for example, if one partner
has renewed but the other has lapsed.

---

## External Systems [H]

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **SiteWorks Activated** | Turn this on if your u3a uses SiteWorks for its website. When activated, Beacon2 hides the **Schedule** tab on group records and the scheduling fields, because scheduling is managed in SiteWorks instead. |

---

## Saving your changes

When you have finished making changes, click the **Update** button at the bottom
of the page. Beacon2 will save all settings in one go.

> **Tip:** Some settings (such as year start date) affect calculations across
> the whole system. Make sure you are confident before changing them, and
> consult your Regional Beacon Champion if unsure.
