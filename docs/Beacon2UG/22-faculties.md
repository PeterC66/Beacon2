# 22. Faculties

Faculties are organisational groupings for your u3a's interest groups. They
let you categorise groups by subject area -- for example "Arts & Crafts",
"Languages", "Science & Technology", or "Physical Activities". Members can
then filter the Groups List by faculty to find groups that interest them.

Faculties are entirely optional. If your u3a does not use them, this page
will simply be empty.

## Opening the Faculties page

From the **Home** page, click **Faculties** in the navigation bar.

![The Faculties page](images/22-faculties.png)

---

## The faculties table

The page shows a simple table listing each faculty by name [A]. If your u3a
has no faculties yet, the table will be empty with a prompt to add one.

---

## Adding a new faculty

At the bottom of the table, type the name of the new faculty into the text
field [B] and click **Add** [C]. The new faculty is saved immediately and
appears in the table.

Once added, the faculty becomes available in the **Faculty** dropdown on the
Group Record Details tab (see [Section 17](17-group-record-details.md)) and
as a filter on the Groups List (see [Section 16](16-groups-list.md)).

---

## Editing a faculty

To rename an existing faculty:

1. Click the **Edit** button [D] next to the faculty you want to change.
2. The name becomes editable -- type the new name.
3. Click **Save** [E] to confirm, or **Cancel** [F] to discard the change.

The new name takes effect everywhere the faculty is used -- you do not need
to update individual group records.

---

## Deleting a faculty

Click the **Delete** button [G] next to the faculty you want to remove. A
confirmation dialog appears warning you that any groups currently assigned to
this faculty will become unassigned.

> **Note:** Deleting a faculty does not delete the groups themselves -- they
> simply lose their faculty assignment and will appear as having no faculty
> in the Groups List.
