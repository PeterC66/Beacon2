# 62. u3a Officers

The **u3a Officers** page lets you manage the committee members, office
holders, and other officials for your u3a. You can record who holds each
office, their contact email, and whether they should receive notifications
when new members join online.

To open it, click **u3a Officers** in the **Misc** section of the Home page.

![The u3a Officers page](images/officers.png)

---

## The officers table

The table [A] lists all defined offices with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Office name** [B] | The title of the office (e.g. "Chair", "Treasurer", "Membership Secretary"). |
| **Post Holder** [C] | The member who currently holds this office. The name is a link -- click it to open the member's record. |
| **Office Email** [D] | An optional email address for this office (this may be different from the post holder's personal email). |
| **Notifications** [E] | A checkbox indicating whether this officer should receive email notifications when a new member joins online. |

---

## Post holder status colouring

The post holder's name is colour-coded to flag members whose status may need
attention:

- **Red with strikethrough** -- the member's status is **Deceased** or
  **Resigned**. You should assign a new post holder.
- **Red text** (no strikethrough) -- the member's status is **Lapsed**. They
  may need to renew their membership.

---

## Adding, editing, and deleting offices

- **Add** [F] -- click the **Add** button to create a new office row. Enter
  the office name, select a post holder from the member list, and optionally
  provide an office email address.

- **Edit** [G] -- click on an existing office row to edit its details inline.
  You can change the office name, select a different post holder, update the
  email, or toggle the notifications checkbox.

- **Delete** [H] -- select the office you wish to remove and click the
  **Delete** button. A confirmation will appear before the office is removed.

---

## Sending bulk email to officers

To send an email to several officers at once:

1. Tick the checkbox next to each officer you wish to contact [I].
2. Click the **Send email** button [J].
3. You will be taken to the email composition screen with the selected
   officers' email addresses pre-filled.

> **Tip:** This is a convenient way to send a message to the whole committee
> without having to look up each person's email address individually.
