# 58. Public Links

The Public Links page is where you configure the public-facing URLs for your
u3a and control what information is visible on your online joining form,
Members Portal, public groups list, and public calendar.

To open this page, click **Public links** in the **Misc** section of the
Home page.

![The Public Links page](images/58-public-links.png)

---

## Member Services [A]

This section shows the URLs that your members and prospective members use to
access your u3a online. Each URL has a **Copy** button so you can easily paste
it into your website or emails.

| Link | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Join form** | The URL for your online joining page, where prospective members can apply to join your u3a |
| **Members Portal** | The URL for the self-service portal where existing members can view their details, renew, and manage their membership |

---

## Public Information [B]

These URLs provide read-only public pages that anyone can visit:

| Link | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Public groups list** | A publicly visible list of your u3a's interest groups |
| **Public calendar** | A publicly visible calendar of upcoming group meetings |

---

## Online Joining Configuration [C]

Control whether prospective members can join your u3a online:

| Setting | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Enable / Disable** | Turn online joining on or off |
| **Privacy policy URL** | The URL of your u3a's privacy policy, shown to applicants during the joining process |
| **PayPal status** | Shows whether PayPal is configured for accepting online payments (set up in [System Settings](48-system-settings.md)) |

---

## Configure Members Portal [D]

Use these checkboxes to control which features are available to members when
they log into the Members Portal:

| Feature | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Renewals** | Members can renew their membership online |
| **Groups** | Members can view group information and join or leave groups |
| **Calendar** | Members can view the calendar of group meetings |
| **Personal details** | Members can view and update their own contact details |
| **Replacement card** | Members can request a replacement membership card |

Untick a feature to hide it from the portal entirely.

---

## Configure Group Information [E]

This section controls what group details are visible on the **members'** view
and the **public** view of the groups list. It is presented as a toggle grid
with a row for each piece of information and a column for each audience:

| Information | Members | Public |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Status** | Whether the group is open, full, etc. | Same |
| **Venue** | Where the group meets | Same |
| **Contact** | The group leader or contact person | Same |
| **Detail** | Additional details about the group | Same |
| **Enquiries** | An enquiry email link | Same |
| **Join group** | A button to join the group | Same |

For each combination, tick the toggle to show that information or untick it to
hide it. You might, for example, show the venue to members but hide it from
the public, or show the contact name to members but only the enquiries link
to the public.

---

## Configure Calendar [F]

Similar to the group information grid above, this section controls what
calendar details are visible to members and to the public:

| Information | Members | Public |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Venue** | Where the meeting takes place | Same |
| **Topic** | The topic or subject for the meeting | Same |
| **Enquiries** | An enquiry email link | Same |
| **Detail** | Additional details about the meeting | Same |
| **Download** | A link to download the calendar entry | Same |

Again, tick or untick each toggle to control visibility for each audience.

> **Tip:** Review these settings periodically, especially if you change your
> website or privacy policy. Make sure the public pages show enough information
> to attract new members without revealing details that should remain private.
