# 14. Membership Statistics

The **Statistics** screen gives you an at-a-glance overview of your u3a's membership
numbers, broken down in several useful ways. It is read-only — there is nothing to
edit here, just useful information for reporting and planning.

To open it, click **Statistics** on the Home page.

![Membership statistics screen](images/14-membership-statistics.png)

---

## General Member Status

The first section [A] shows headline figures about your membership:

| Statistic | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Year start** | The date your current membership year began |
| **Renewal period** | The date range during which renewals are expected |
| **Grace / lapse period** | The period after the renewal deadline during which members are given extra time before being lapsed |
| **Current not renewed** | The number of current members whose renewal date has passed but who have not yet renewed |
| **Lapsed count** | The total number of members with a Lapsed status |

This section is particularly useful during the renewal season to track how many
members still need to renew.

---

## Current Members by Class

The second section [B] breaks down your current (active) membership by class:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **Class** | The membership class name (e.g. Individual, Joint, Life) |
| **Count** | Total number of current members in that class |
| **Percentage** | What proportion of total membership this class represents |
| **With email** | How many members in the class have an email address on file |
| **Without email** | How many do not have an email address |
| **First year** | Members in their first year of membership (joined during the current year) |
| **Second year** | Members in their second year of membership |

> **Tip:** The "With email" and "Without email" columns are helpful when planning
> communications. Members without email will need letters or phone calls instead.

---

## Active Groups

The third section [C] summarises your u3a's interest groups:

| Statistic | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Active groups** | The total number of groups that are currently active |
| **Average members** | The average number of members per group |
| **Members not in any group** | How many current members do not belong to any group |

> **Tip:** A high number of members not in any group might indicate an opportunity
> to encourage more participation, or it may simply reflect members who attend
> open meetings only.

---

## Members by Renew Date

The fourth section [D] shows membership numbers filtered by renewal date range.
Use the date selectors [E] to choose a start and end date, then view:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **Renewed** | Members who renewed their membership within the selected date range |
| **New** | Members who joined as new members within the selected date range |

This is useful for reporting to your committee — for example, "In January we had
15 renewals and 3 new members."

---

## Download and print

You can download or print the statistics for use in committee reports:

- Click **Download** [F] to save the statistics as a file.
- Click **Print** [G] to send them to your printer.
