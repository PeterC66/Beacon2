# 54. Member Statuses

Member Statuses define the lifecycle states a member can be in — from active
membership through to lapsed, resigned, or deceased. Beacon2 comes with a set of
system statuses that cannot be changed, and you can add your own custom statuses
if needed.

To open this page, click **Member statuses** in the **Set Up** section of the
Home page.

![The Member Statuses page](images/54-member-statuses.png)

---

## System statuses [A]

The following statuses are built into Beacon2 and cannot be renamed or deleted:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Current** | An active, paid-up member |
| **Lapsed** | A member who has not renewed by the grace period deadline |
| **Resigned** | A member who has formally resigned |
| **Deceased** | A member who has passed away |
| **Applicant** | A prospective member who has applied but is not yet confirmed |

These statuses are shown as **read-only** rows in the table.

---

## Custom statuses [B]

If the system statuses are not enough for your u3a's needs, you can create your
own. For example, some u3as add a "Suspended" or "Life Member" status.

### Adding a custom status

Type a name in the **Add new status** field at the bottom of the list and click
**Add**.

### Renaming a custom status

Click the status name to edit it inline, then save your change.

### Deleting a custom status

Click **Delete** next to a custom status to remove it. You can only delete a
status that is not currently assigned to any members.

> **Tip:** Keep the number of custom statuses small. Too many statuses can make
> reporting and filtering confusing. In most cases the built-in statuses are
> sufficient.
