# 11. Non-Renewals

The **Non-renewals** screen helps you identify members who have not renewed their
membership so you can decide what action to take — whether to chase them up, mark
them as lapsed, or eventually remove them from the system.

To open it, click **Non-renewals** on the Home page.

![Non-renewals screen](images/11-non-renewals.png)

---

## Choosing a mode

The screen has two modes, selected by the toggle at the top [A]:

- **This year** — shows members whose renewal date has passed during the current
  membership period but who have not yet renewed. These are your "recently overdue"
  members who may still come back.
- **Long-term** — shows members who have not renewed for a specified number of years.
  Use the year selector [B] to choose how far back to look (e.g. 2 years, 3 years).
  These are members who are unlikely to return and may be candidates for deletion.

---

## The non-renewals table

The table displays one row per non-renewed member with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Select** | Checkbox for selecting members for bulk actions [C] |
| **Membership No** | The member's membership number (click to open their record) |
| **Name** | Full name of the member |
| **Class** | Their membership class (e.g. Individual, Joint) |
| **Status** | Current membership status |
| **Next renewal** | The renewal date that was missed |
| **Email** | The member's email address, if known |

You can sort the table by clicking any column header, and use the **Select all**
checkbox at the top of the selection column to select every member in the list.

---

## Bulk actions

### This year mode — Lapse selected

In **This year** mode, the main action button is **Lapse selected** [D]. This changes
the status of all selected members to **Lapsed**.

Use this when the renewal period (including any grace period) has passed and the member
has not renewed. Lapsing a member keeps their record in the system but marks them as
no longer current.

> **Grace period:** The screen shows the grace period that applies to your u3a at the
> top of the page [E]. Members are not normally lapsed until the grace period has
> expired — this gives them extra time to renew after their renewal date.

### Long-term mode — Delete selected

In **Long-term** mode, the main action button is **Delete selected** [F]. This
permanently removes the selected member records from the system.

> **Gift Aid warning:** Do **not** delete a member if your u3a has claimed Gift Aid
> on their subscription within the last 7 years. HMRC requires you to keep records
> of Gift Aid donors for 7 years. The screen displays a warning about this [G].
> If in doubt, leave the member in the system as Lapsed rather than deleting them.

---

## When to use Resigned, Lapsed, or Deceased

Beacon2 provides several statuses for members who are no longer current. Here is
guidance on which to use:

- **Resigned** — The member has actively told you they do not wish to continue.
  Set their status to Resigned on their member record.
- **Lapsed** — The member simply did not renew and has not been in contact. Use
  the **Lapse selected** button on this screen, or change their status manually.
- **Deceased** — The member has passed away. Set their status to Deceased on their
  member record. This is important for accurate reporting and to avoid sending
  correspondence to the member.

> **Tip:** You do not need to delete Resigned or Lapsed members straight away.
> Keeping them in the system for a while means you can still see their history
> and re-activate them if they decide to come back.

---

## Typical workflow

1. Early in the renewal period, use **This year** mode to see who has not yet renewed.
2. Contact those members (by email or letter) to remind them.
3. Once the grace period has expired, select the remaining non-renewers and click
   **Lapse selected**.
4. Periodically, switch to **Long-term** mode to review members who have been lapsed
   for several years.
5. For long-term lapsed members with no Gift Aid concern, select them and click
   **Delete selected** to tidy up your records.
