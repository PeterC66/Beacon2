# 3. Changing Your Password

When you first log in to Beacon2 -- or after your password has been reset by a
Site Administrator -- you will be taken to the **Change Password** screen before
you can access any other part of the system. You must set a new password and a
security question before continuing.

![The Change Password screen](images/change-password.png)

## Password requirements

Your new password must meet **all** of the following rules:

- Between **10 and 72 characters** long
- **No spaces**
- Must include at least one **uppercase** letter (A-Z)
- Must include at least one **lowercase** letter (a-z)
- Must include at least one **number** (0-9)

You may also use the special characters `! @ # $ % ^ & *` if you wish, but
they are not required.

> **Tip:** Do not use common words or easily guessable information. A good
> approach is to think of a short phrase and mix in some numbers, for example
> `MyU3aClub2025!`.

## Filling in the form

1. **New password** [A] -- type your chosen password. As you type, Beacon2
   will show hints telling you whether your password meets the requirements.
   A green message "Password meets requirements" confirms it is valid.

2. **Confirm** [B] -- type the same password again. A green message "Passwords
   match" will appear when the two entries are identical.

3. **Question** [C] -- a default question ("Your first school") is provided.
   You can change this to any question you like. Choose something whose answer
   you will remember but that other people are unlikely to know.

4. **Answer** [D] -- type the answer to your chosen question. **Remember the
   exact format** you use (including capitalisation), because you will need to
   type it the same way if you ever use the password recovery flow.

5. Press **Submit** [E] to save your new password. You will then be taken to
   the Beacon2 Home page.

> If you do not want to proceed, you can click **Log out instead** below the
> form to return to the login page.

## Changing your password later

You can change your password at any time from the **Personal Preferences**
page (see [Section 60](60-personal-preferences.md)), accessible from the
**Misc** section of the Home page.
